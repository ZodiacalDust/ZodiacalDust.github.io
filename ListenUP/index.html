July 17, London
-------------------

I went to Abbey Road, didnt take any photo cuz MY PHONE WAS DEAD.
Fortunately, Brian May was in Abbey Road Studio, having a party celebrating the remastered album 'Star Fleet'

I went to about 8 CD stores, all sold out. Finally bought one in the 9th store I visited.

I have to say, this is a fun album, I'll rate it as A+.
Salute to Brian & EVH for their awesome Blues & Rock jams in this album :)

It was about 6PM, the party was over.
Outside the studio, Brian signed on my hoddie :)
WHAT A NICE GUY!
I told him that I am a Physics student inspired by him.
He seemed surprised and shook my hand.

BTW, his driver Phil, a retired British Royal Navy officer, is also soooooo NICE!
He is a guy with a great sense of humor XD! He chated with me outside the studio and taught me how to salute like a soldier :-)
